Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about donation on our platform.

How do I begin donating or requesting items on the website?

You must register or log in via the Login/Signup page. The website uses local storage to track your session, and accessing protected pages like dashboards or listings will prompt you to log in if you haven't already.

As a recipient, how do I request a donation?

You navigate to the Recipient Dashboard. From there, click the "Post New Request" card to open the request form. You will fill in details like the item name, category, quantity, your name, contact, and address, then click "Submit Request".

Where can I see a list of items that are needed?

All active donation requests are listed on the Donation Listings page. You can browse all requests or use the search bar and category filters (e.g., Clothes, Food, Medicine) to find specific items requested by recipients.

How do I make a donation to a request?

On the Donation Listings page, click the "Donate" button next to the item. A "Confirm Donation" modal will appear where you enter your Donor Name, Contact (phone/email), Quantity, and your preferred Delivery Option (Drop-off, Meet-up, or Pick-up). Submitting this creates a new donation record.

Can I request multiple donations at once?

Yes. You can request multiple items, but each request must be submitted separately.

How does the website handle the status of requests and donations?

Requests start as 'Active'. When a donor commits an item, the Request status may change to 'Pending' or 'In progress'. Donations start as 'Pending'. Donors can later use their dashboard to update the status, and recipients can view the progress on the Donation Status screen.

Will I be notified if my request gets a match or if a donor changes a status?

Yes. The system creates notifications for all users when a new request is posted, notifies recipients of a Donation Match, and alerts both donors and recipients of status updates (e.g., changing from 'Pending' to 'Completed'). You can view them on the Notification Center.

Do I need to switch accounts if I want to donate and request items?

No. You can use the same account for both actions.

I forgot my login details. How can I reset my password?

On the Logi/Signup page, click the "Forgot your password? Reset" button. You will be prompted to enter your email. If your email is registered, the system will display a success message indicating a password reset link has been "sent".

Where can I find more information about AlayAkap?

You can visit the About Us page to read about the mission, vision, and core values. The Contact Us page provides a form, email address and phone number.
